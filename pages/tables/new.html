Gfinity announces $100,000 event


2015-07-23

Called the Gfinity Champion of Champions, the tournament will take place from September 26-27 as part of EGX 2015, the biggest games event in the United Kingdom.

The top four teams from the previous Gfinity events, fnatic, Ninjas in Pyjamas, EnVyUs and Virtus.pro, will face off for the title of Gfinity Champions 2015 and the bulk of the $100,000 prize pool.
